 2.Project Overview
     This project analyzes the annual sales performance of Aditi Store for the year 2022 using an interactive Excel dashboard.  
     The dashboard provides insights into sales trends, customer demographics, order status, and channel performance to support business decision-making.

3. Dataset
- Annual sales data for the year 2022
- Includes order details, sales amount, customer demographics, sales channels, product categories, and order status

4. KPIs & Metrics
- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Monthly Orders vs Sales Trend
- Sales by Gender (Men vs Women)
- Order Status Distribution
- Top 5 States by Sales
- Orders by Age Group & Gender
- Orders by Sales Channel

5. Key Insights
- Women contribute a higher share of total sales compared to men
- Amazon and Myntra are the top-performing sales channels
- Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh generate the highest revenue
- Over 90% of orders are successfully delivered
- Adult customers show the highest purchasing activity

6. Dashboard Features
- Interactive slicers for Month, Channel, and Category
- Dynamic charts for trend and distribution analysis
- Clean and user-friendly dashboard layout

7. Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
